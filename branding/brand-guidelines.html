01 — Overview

Who we are.

KOMISSIO is the AI operating system for commission-driven businesses in Dubai and the UAE. We sit beneath existing tools — WhatsApp, Excel, local ERPs, CRMs — and turn sales activity, accounting data, legal contracts, and property operations into one live, reconciled system.

Our brand must feel like the company we are: serious, technical, and built specifically for the UAE. Not a Silicon Valley import. Not a generic SaaS product. Infrastructure for a city that moves fast.

Brand Personality

Confident
We know the problem deeply. We have conviction in the solution. Our voice doesn’t hedge.
Technical
We speak to operators who live in Excel and WhatsApp. Precision matters. Vague AI claims are not our style.
Local
DLD, Ejari, VAT, Musataha — the UAE context is not an afterthought. It is the product.

Brand Promise

“Not another CRM. Not another ERP. KOMISSIO is business infrastructure for the UAE.”

02 — Logo & Mark

The Mark

The KOMISSIO mark is a three-element geometric system representing the OS architecture: a full-width bar (the operating system layer beneath all tools), with two modules below — left in teal (operational) and right in gold (commission, value).

Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the “O” in KOMISSIO around the logo on all sides. Never crowd the logo with other elements.

✓ Do
Use the provided logo files. Maintain proportions. Use on approved backgrounds.
✗ Don’t
Stretch, rotate, recolor, add shadows, add outlines, or place on busy photographic backgrounds.

05 — Brand Voice

How we speak.

We say

“KOMISSIO maps every deal to a journal entry automatically.”
“Commission disputes drop to zero when there’s one source of truth.”
“Your Ejari renewals, 30 days before they expire.”
“One system. Four roles. Real-time.”

We don’t say

“Leverage AI-powered insights to unlock synergies.”
“The all-in-one platform for everything.”
“Disruptive solution revolutionizing the space.”
“10x your business with KOMISSIO.”

Naming Conventions

KOMISSIO
Always all-caps in display use. Sentence case allowed in body copy.
KOMISSIO OS
Refers to the full operating system product.
Komissio
Acceptable in flowing text. Never “komissio” (all lower).
