# KOMISSIO Logo Usage Guidelines

## The Mark

The KOMISSIO mark is a three-element geometric symbol representing the operating system metaphor:

- **Top bar (full width)** — The OS layer beneath all tools. Rendered in Deep Teal (#0D5C63) on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds.
- **Bottom-left module** — The operational layer (sales, legal, property). Rendered in Deep Teal at 55% opacity.
- **Bottom-right module** — The commission and value layer. Always rendered in Commission Gold (#C9A84C).

The Gold bottom-right element is the only element that may **never** change colour. It is the brand's most recognisable anchor.

---

## Clear Space

Always maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the mark element on all sides. Do not crowd the logo with text, imagery, or other brand marks.

## Minimum Size

- **Logo lockup (mark + wordmark):** 120px wide / 32mm print minimum
- **Mark only:** 24px / 6mm print minimum
- **Wordmark only:** 100px / 28mm print minimum

| Teal lockup | Deep Teal #0D5C63 |
| Dark ink | White, very light surfaces only |

---

## What NOT to Do

- Do not rotate the mark
- Do not stretch or distort any element
- Do not change the Gold (#C9A84C) bottom-right module to any other colour
- Do not place the primary logo on dark backgrounds (use reversed version)
- Do not add drop shadows, gradients, or effects to the mark
- Do not use the mark at less than 24px
- Do not separate the mark from the wordmark in the lockup without approval
- Do not place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds
- Do not outline or stroke the mark elements

---

## File Formats

- **SVG** — Preferred for all digital use. Fully scalable, no quality loss.
- **PNG** — Use for contexts that do not support SVG (some email clients, social media upload tools). Export from the SVG at 2x or 3x your target size.
- **PDF** — Use for print production. Export from SVG at the required physical dimensions.

To export PNG from SVG:
1. Open the SVG in Figma, Illustrator, or Inkscape
2. Set export dimensions (e.g., 480×480px for a 2x icon)
3. Export as PNG with transparent background
